Coolfridge Hire & Lease?

We offer short term hire or medium term lease for businesses or individuals requiring on-site chilled or frozen storage.

All equipment utilizes the latest technology, quick and easy to set up and operate.

Who uses a Coolfridge Hire & Lease?

Beverage, hospitality and food manufacturing businesses.

Established operators needing to upsize their existing storage capacity.

Operators wanting turn-key chilling and freezing solutions.

Where can a Coolfridge Hire & Lease room be installed?

Inside new and existing buildings, outdoor seervice areas and public or corporate events.

We also offer solutions for locations where mains power supply is not available.

Specifications

  • Sizes: Sizes to suit most applications
  • Construction: White steel skin panels with high density polyurethane foam core. No CFC or HCFC.
  • Power: 230 v 10amp 3 pin supply & 400 v 3 phase supply
  • Thermostat: Electronic, LED Display
  • Drain: Evaporative. No mains drain required.
  • Floor: Heavy duty aluminium tread plate
  • Door: Hinged self-closing, lockable, internal safety release door latch
  • Door Frame: Heated (freezer only)
  • Lighting: Interior with switch at door
  • Shelving: Adjustable, 4 tiers (optional)
